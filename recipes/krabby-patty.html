Krabby Patty

Vote best burger in Bikini Bottom

The preferred meal of Bikini Bottom residents

Only using the finest Wagyu Beef cuts available

  • 1lb Wagyu Beef
  • 2 slice American Cheese
  • Barnacle shavings
  • Sesame seed bun
  • Pickles
  • Mustard
  • Ketchup
  • Onions

Recipe instructions

    Cook Wagyu beef to a medium rare
    Add 2 slices of american cheese to a melt on the patty
    Spread ketchup & mustard on sesame seed bun
    Place pickles & onions and is ready to serve
