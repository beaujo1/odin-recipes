Secret ramen recipe hidden for thousands of years

Only using the finest homemadenoodles Japan has to offer

  • Chicken breast
  • Shiitake mushrooms

Recipe Instructions

    Bring 2 cups of water to roaring boil
    Sear chicken (both sides) then add to boiling water
    Add shiitake mushrooms
    Add 2 brown eggs
  • Add chicken stock cook for 69 minutes then enjoy with a glass of Sake
